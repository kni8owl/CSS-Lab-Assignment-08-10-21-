This is a demonstration of CSS Box-model.
The white gap from the dark grey navigation bar to each side of the Box-1 is the margin of 70px.
The purple section is the border of Box-1 of 20px.
The blue gap from the border to the 'Box 1' text is the padding of 40px.
This is the overall Box model of CSS.
How Padding is done is nicely shown in the Box-2.
Also, I have given 70px margin to Box-1 and 30px margin to Box-2, but the gap between Box-1 and Box-2 is not 100px (70px + 30px), instead, its 70 px.
This is due to Margin collapse, which considers the greater margin out of the two boxes.
